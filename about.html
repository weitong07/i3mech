Our Story

Founded in 2004 by two Malaysian and one Norwegian shareholder, iTrimech is a niche Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) provider specializing in advanced electrical and electronic. Headquartered in Puchong, Malaysia, with regional offices in Malaysia (ASEAN) and Norway (Europe), our team of professionals delivers innovative, high-quality, and customized EMS solutions to meet diverse industry needs. Guided by our core values Innovative, Involving, and Integrity we take pride in our ability to serve specialized markets with precision engineering and manufacturing excellence.
